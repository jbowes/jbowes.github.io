Keane is Krazy

#fedora

Got an email to my Comp Sci account from a Keane recruiter
today. It was very generic; my name wasn’t in the email, and it said they were
hiring for basically every position under the sun, and my skills matched those.
I sent a reply saying that I was happily employed and no longer living in the
area, but thanks for asking and good luck in your search!

The email was total spam, and even if I was looking for a job the nature of this email would have completely turned me off. I can’t see how they think such tactics will net them any employees of any worth.
